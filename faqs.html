FAQs

1.WHAT SERVICES DO YOU PROVIDE?

      SPREAD SMILES CHARITY acts as a bridge between the donor and beneficiaries for the underprivileged Indian population's welfare regarding education and other basic needs.

2.FOR LOOKING A SERVICES WHOM SHOULD I CONTACT?

      You can contact our team members or send us an email.

3.WHAT KIND OF DONATIONS DO YOU ACCEPT?

      Currently, we are accepting only the cash donations as most of our clients need money to run their family and for education.

4.I AM INTERESTED IN VOLUNTEERING?

      Please send an email to this address, we will contact you.

5.CAN I GET A DONOR TO SPONSOR MYSELF TO A STUDENT HIGHER EDUCATION?

      Definitely, yes. The amount you can pay to the charity and we will direct it to the college. All information provided will be kept confidential and shared with the donors.

6.IS SPREAD SMILES CHARITY AN INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATION?

      No, Spread Smiles Charity is a non profitable trust, which currently supports the indian community.

7.WILL MY SMALL CONTRIBUTION MAKE A DIFFERENCE?

      Yes, definitely 90% of our contributions are a small amount, and those small contributions will definitely make a big impact on society.

8.​HOW DOES SPREAD SMILES CHARITY CHOOSE THE PROJECTS IT SUPPORTS?

      Our charity's development support team works closely with partners to understand the beneficiaries' needs and run the complete analysis by doing a background check before supporting them.

9.WHAT IS THE HELP DONE BY SPREAD SMILES CHARITY?

      We have been continuously supporting and providing funds for families and students for their education and daily living. Moreover, we are funding to build a house for the homeless and provide funds for disability and hospital treatment.

10.DOES SPREAD SMILES CHARITY RECEIVE GOVERNMENT FUNDING?

      No, we don't receive any funds from the government or not even from any private organizations.
