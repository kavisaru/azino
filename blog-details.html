Spread smiles by lifting others

In general an altitude of kindness and understanding towards others, now especially suggesting generosity. Not everyone is lucky enough to have a lot of toys or even a warm coat for winter. Sometimes people haven't been able to earn money because they can't find a job. Other people are sick or elderly and can’t get around on their own. Charity helps the people whom they need. You work hard, you make money, you do it for yourself, that is not a life. You go out, you seek for the people who need your help. You makes their life better you become that sponge get that absorbs all negativity and you become the person emit beautiful posing Vibes and when you realize that you have changed someone's life and because of you this person didn’t give up Make Charity a part of your life and help make the world a better place to live.

Charity is the willingness to help others. It is one of the noble tasks that a man can do in his life. It is to help people when you know then can’t help you back. Charity is a building built on the basement of love and sympathy. The purpose of charity is to help people in need. By cultivating this noble quality, we find to share love and happiness. It brings peace of mind to the person who receives and the one who gives. It encourages the human hearts to spread the message of brotherhood and love among the people. Each human has his own needs. He may not be able to make up to his needs because of his ailments , poverty and other measures. Thus by the act of giving One can lift up others' life. One cultivates the habit of being kind. In this Life we can't do great things, we can do only small things with great love. Let's join hands to spread the language of love to this entire world such that the entire human race is your extended family....
