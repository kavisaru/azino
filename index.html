What we do

Spread Smiles Charity

Social welfare

We provide assistance to individuals and families through programs such as healthcare, food stamps, unemployment compensation, housing assistance and child care assistance.

Donate Now

Education

Even today millions of children in India are being denied from education. Our motive is at least help a few children from the denial of their education to bring this injustice to an end.

Donate Now

Health & Disability support

We work directly with the families who have a relative with cognitive disabilities and help them to see the difference it can make to the whole family when the disabled individual finds, and plays their part in family life.

Donate Now

Old Age

Our Spread Smiles Charity provides support to older people to live fulfilled lives. We aspire to a society where all older people are treated with respect and have the opportunity to live fulfilled lives.

How we do

How Spread Smiles Charity Works ?.

Our volunteers/friends find people in need or projects that are sustainable, cost effective and that create self-reliance in their communities. An application is submitted.

Application

Board members carry out thorough assessment of application including personal visits by other volunteers. If valid and supportable, a project is formed ​and assigned to a team of volunteers from nearby communities to implement the project.

Project

The charity will not pay any compensation to our members and co-workers. Your gift will solely be used for the support of in need.

Terms

Work closely with volunteers to monitor progress and ensure common objectives are achieved.
