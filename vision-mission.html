Vision

Our vision is to Spread Smiles by Lifting Others.


Mission

Spread Smiles charity's mission is to bring God's love into action by bringing people together to aid education, poverty, optimal wellness, participate and contribute economically for the most underprivileged families for their basic needs without distinction of caste,creed or color .


Objectives

1. TO FOCUS ON CHILDREN FOR PURSUING THEIR HIGHER EDUCATION

2. TO FOCUS ON ELDERLY PEOPLE FOR THEIR FINANCIAL WELL-BEING

3. FOCUSING ON PEOPLE WITH DISABILITIES AND HEALTH PROBLEMS

Spread smiles by lifting others.
