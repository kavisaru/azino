Social welfare

We provide assistance to individuals and families through programs such as healthcare, food stamps, unemployment compensation, housing assistance and child care assistance. The factors involved can include the size of the family unit, current income level, or an assessed disability.

In short we provide financial or other aid to individuals or groups who cannot support themselves.


Education

Even today millions of children in India are being denied a chance at an enabling education and therefore a happy childhood. This happens mainly because of who they are and where they are born. Our motive is atlas helps a few children from the denial of their education to bring this injustice to an end.

The amount donated by each person translates into getting one more child into the world of education. Your support qips save the children to roll out on ground projects that not only get more children enrolled into education, but also ensure they remain there and get holistic and quality education.


Health & Disability support

We work directly with the families who have a relative with all cognitive disabilities and help them to see the difference it can make to the whole family when the disabled individual finds, and plays their part in family life. Often those affected by cognitive disabilities can be such a burden to their families, as there is a very little expectation that they can do anything of any help. All individuals have the ability to learn something, and so we expect family and show them the possible capability of their disabled relative and so they are less of a burden and more of a valued family member.


Old Age

Our Spread Smiles Charity provides support to older people to live fulfilled lives.

We aspire to the society where all older people are treated with respect and have the opportunity to live fulfilled lives. We donate funds for the Old Age homes and especially for the people with health disabilities like blind and people who Don't have any financial support from their families.


Environmental Protection

We also aim to protect our environment, because environmental degradation was also one of the main causes of poverty. Those blessed with a little extra can support a worthy cause, and the donation can be given in the name of a chosen gift recipient.
